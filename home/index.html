I’m Don, a French multidisciplinary artist and creative producer based in Cork, Ireland, with over a decade of experience in video production, photography, and editing. I create content that fuses visual storytelling with artistic impact, working with small businesses, personal brands, and cultural organizations to craft compelling photo and video content for websites, campaigns, and social media platforms. My work spans long- and short-form story-driven videos, professional photography, and collage-based visuals, often blending analog textures with digital fluency. As the founder of Poetonic, I produce original art-led content that resonates with online audiences, drawing from a rich background in traditional media and web3 culture.
