Collage based visuals – Don Samec

At Poetonic, collage is more than an art form—it’s a powerful medium to convey meaningful stories and ideas. Since 2021, I’ve built a community of over 2,000 collage enthusiasts and created more than 1,000 digital artworks collected by over 260 supporters worldwide. My work has been exhibited internationally—from Belgium to New York—and featured in collaborations with leading Web3 brands like DRiP Haus and Sleepagotchi Inc. Using a mix of video, reels, infographics, and writing, I craft and share content that educates and inspires others to engage deeply with collage as a tool for visual storytelling and creative expression.
