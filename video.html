Videos

Over the years, I’ve produced a wide range of video work spanning commercial shoots, music videos, short films, and event coverage—including vibrant moments from Cork’s St. Patrick’s Day parade. My video practice is deeply intertwined with my collage work; on the Poetonic YouTube channel, I regularly share educational and behind-the-scenes content exploring the creative process of analog collage. Meanwhile, the Don Samec channel showcases a broader mix of documentary, creative, and commercial video projects. Whether you're looking for artistic insight or visual storytelling, I invite you to explore both channels.
